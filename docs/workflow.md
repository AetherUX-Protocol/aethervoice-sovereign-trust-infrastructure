# AetherVoice Workflow

## Trust-Aware Verification & Confidential Settlement Flow

---

# Workflow Overview

AetherVoice enables businesses, brokers, vendors, and autonomous agents to verify high-risk transactions before settlement occurs.

The workflow combines:

* SNS persistent identity
* QVAC local AI verification
* Umbra confidential settlement

into a unified trust-aware transaction system.

The platform is designed for:

* sensitive commerce
* procurement workflows
* stablecoin settlements
* autonomous commerce systems
* high-risk business transactions

---

---

# Step 1 — Identity Initialization

The workflow begins when a user or autonomous agent connects:

* wallet
* SNS identity (.sol)
* transaction session

AetherVoice creates a:

# persistent trust profile

associated with the participant.

The SNS identity acts as:

* the identity anchor
* reputation reference
* trust continuity layer

---

# Step 2 — Transaction Upload

The participant uploads transaction materials such as:

* invoices
* contracts
* payment instructions
* broker communications
* LOIs
* screenshots
* procurement documents
* WhatsApp exports

These files remain local during verification.

---

# Step 3 — Local AI Verification (QVAC)

QVAC-powered local AI processes uploaded transaction materials.

The verification pipeline includes:

## OCR Extraction

Extracts text and structured information from uploaded documents.

## Semantic Analysis

Analyzes communication consistency and contextual meaning.

## Anomaly Detection

Identifies:

* suspicious payment changes
* urgency manipulation
* inconsistent formatting
* unusual transaction behavior

## Authenticity Review

Evaluates credibility indicators across transaction materials.

All analysis occurs locally.

Sensitive documents never leave the device.

---

# Step 4 — Trust Evaluation

The AetherVoice trust engine generates:

* trust score
* authenticity confidence
* risk indicators
* transaction recommendation

Possible outputs include:

## Proceed

Transaction appears consistent and trustworthy.

## Caution

Potential inconsistencies or elevated risk detected.

## Stop

High-risk indicators or suspicious behavior identified.

---

# Step 5 — Confidential Settlement (Umbra)

If trust conditions are satisfied:

* settlement proceeds privately through Umbra
* balances remain confidential
* counterparties stay shielded
* transaction exposure is minimized

This protects:

* commercial relationships
* settlement amounts
* payment activity
* business intelligence

from public blockchain visibility.

---

# Step 6 — Trust Identity Update

After transaction completion, AetherVoice updates the participant’s SNS trust profile.

Trust signals may include:

* successful settlements
* verification consistency
* transaction reliability
* behavioral credibility
* authenticity history

This creates:

# persistent economic trust identity.
